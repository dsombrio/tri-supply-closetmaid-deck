Wire Systems

Fixed Mount Ventilated Wire

Cost-Effective, Practical & Built to Last

Fixed Mount Ventilated Wire systems are cost-effective, practical, and built to last. The open design allows for better airflow — essential for certain environments. ClosetMaid® steel ventilated wire shelves are cold-drawn, significantly enhancing tensile strength. Every wire intersection is welded, with a vinyl coating that fully covers both wires and welds — offering superior protection against corrosion, chipping, and rust.

  • Open-wire design allows better airflow in any environment
  • Cold-drawn steel with welded wire intersections for superior strength
  • Full vinyl coating — resists rust and corrosion
  • Steel components are highly recyclable
  • Most economical system in the ClosetMaid Pro lineup
  • Available in White and Satin Chrome finishes

MaxLoad®

Heavy Duty Wire Closet & Storage Systems

MaxLoad® provides unmatched strength and durability for your storage requirements. This high-performance system combines Heavy Duty shelving with the adjustable ShelfTrack® system — when standards are stud mounted no more than 16” apart, Heavy Duty shelving supports up to 100 lbs. per linear foot. Ideal for storing large items in the garage such as totes, power tools, and lawn equipment.

  • Excellent alternative to standard fixed-shelf applications
